Recruiter (IT and/or Engineering) - Buffalo

Description:

Techladder Inc is a Consulting & Staffing Company established in 1999 with locations in Rochester, NY and Jersey City, NJ. We provide the highest quality of full lifecycle IT, Engineering and Industrial Skilled Trades Staffing services. We provide solutions across a broad range of business and technical platforms. TECHLADDER is a six time Rochester Top 100 and Inc. 5000 winner.
We are searching for an exceptional recruiter to join our Buffalo/Rochester team. We are looking for an individual who is smart, honest, personable, dynamic, competitive, organized, motivated and a team-player with positive energy. We have an excellent team and looking to expand upon that with true professionals. We pride ourselves on hiring based on the person.

Responsibilities

At TECHLADDER you will manage the entire recruitment process for both consulting and full-time positions in the Engineering, Manufacturing/Skilled Trades, Construction and IT marketplace. Working across all industries with some of the best Fortune 5000 clients in the Upstate NY area, you will be responsible for sourcing, submitting candidates, coordination of the interview process and negotiating and closing deals.

Requirements

MUST HAVE 2-10 years of current Staffing industry experience in the Engineering, Manufacturing or IT domain and currently reside in the Rochester area. Must have used LinkedIn extensively for recruiting. We are not a micro-managed, metrics driven firm. We need a highly organized, resourceful, and self motivated team player who can get creative and think outside-the-box to find potential new candidates. Be part of a successful, growing, hardworking team and actually enjoy going to work every day. If you are interested in learning more about Techladder Inc then please send us your resume along with compensation requirements.

Environment

You will be working out of our Buffalo and/or Rochester NY office. Our office has a fun, supportive and collaborative atmosphere. We are a group of seasoned professionals working together to achieve a common goal.
